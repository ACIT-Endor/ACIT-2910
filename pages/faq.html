When was Endor Eats Founded?
Endor Eats was founded in a crowded room of a building in a galaxy not so far away. Our passion for food lead us on a path to create Endor Eats so that we could share our suffering and tears to the rest of the galaxy.
What are your hours of operation?
We work when we want to, nobody tells us what to do. We're open when we're open, deal with it.
Why are some of your staff proffesional looking, and some are just a bowl of ice-cream?
We are proud of our cultural and race diversity. We hire anyone of any race regardless of there appearance. So you should be ashamed for thinking a bowl of ice-cream is any less capable than Mr.Bejaman.
What should I order?
Everything tastes amazing!! (Subjectively speaking), if you don't like anything thats your fault. Go get your tastebuds re-trained. Also try to order only one thing, we only have one chef in-house at all times.
